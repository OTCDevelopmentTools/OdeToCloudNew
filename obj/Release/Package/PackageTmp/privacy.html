Privacy Policy

Standards of Data Security

Your data will never be released to any third party outside of OdeToCloud Solutions without your express written consent, under any circumstances. Any database backups containing your data are stored in a secure system and used solely to support your implementation of our products and services. Upon termination of our project support, or upon your written request as a customer, OdeToCloud Solutions will destroy all data we maintain pertaining to your information.

Data Sales and Distribution

OdeToCloud Solutions never provides customer data or information free of charge, nor does it sell such data to third parties. All terms related to product purchases, maintenance agreements, and enhancements are held in strict confidence.

Physical Security Measures

OdeToCloud Solutions is committed to protecting its data and customers through continuous, 24-hour surveillance and site monitoring at all facilities where sensitive information is stored. Our data centers are equipped with round-the-clock video camera surveillance, on-site security personnel, biometric scanners, and comprehensive visitor logs.

Refund Policy

OdeToCloud Solutions is committed to providing and delivering our services on time. However, if we fail to meet any contractual commitments or deadlines specified in the signed contract, we will consider issuing a prompt refund. Please note that any refund is subject solely to the terms outlined in the signed contract.

Questions or Comments

If you have any questions or comments regarding our Security Policy, please feel free to call us or contact us by email at support@dutechnosys.com.
